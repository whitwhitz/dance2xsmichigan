About 2XS

Dance 2XS Michigan is an student urban dance group at the University of Michigan. It is one of four collegiate chapters of the international dance company, 2XS International, with sister chapters at other universities including UIUC and Purdue. We have sold out the past 3 shows HAHA we're awesome.
